MVF1 - Login and Registration

As a customer:
I want to login to the website
so that I can use all the features of this site.

MVF2 - Online Game

As a customer:
I want to play web game,
so that I can get discount for my purchase items

MVF2.1 - Music (Feature in the Online Game)

As a customer:
I can change the sound level of game music
so that they won't be disturbed by game music

MVF3 - Blog

As a customer:
I want to have a discussion with other clients,
so that I can go to blog page to share my opinion and ideas with others.

MVF4 - Purchase Products

As a customer:
I want to enter the discount code(coupon code),
so that I can pay less when i check out.

MVF5 - Ranking List

As a customer:
I want to check my ranking
so that I konw my ranking, if i'm in the top 3, i can make sure how much discount i get.

MVF 6 - PRODUCT SELLING PAGE

As a customer:
I want to find different games which can apply the coupon code
so that I can choose my desired game and pay for it with less money.

Diagram

Our project is based on marketing, we are aiming to find a way which can attract customers and boost the sale of a gaming store website. We can see that there are lots of gaming store website such as EB Games, The Gamesmen and etc. However, the only things they sell are products itself, there is not much interaction with their customers, customers can only go to the website and buy games or just leave the website. Our innovation is to attract customer to our website by giving them a discount, and how are we going to do that? we got an innovative idea. Our idea is to create a game if they can win the game and get the top 3 on the ranking list, they will get different level of coupon discount code, which they can apply to their purchase item and then get a discount from it.
